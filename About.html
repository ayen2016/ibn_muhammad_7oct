Who is Ibn Muhammad?

In Arabic, Ibn Muhammad means the son of Muhammad. Written works published on this website are under the pseudonym of Ibn Muhammad to work with minimal hype and a sense of mystery.

This platform explores topics that are often left unspoken in society, offering insights into relationships, family, personal growth, and life experiences.

The goal is to encourage reflection, dialogue, and empathy. Ibn Muhammad presents personal perspectives based on real-life experiences.

All writings are personal opinions and aim to share understanding, learning, and awareness, with basic ethical values and respect at the core.
